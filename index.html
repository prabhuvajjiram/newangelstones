Products & Services

Granite Monuments & Headstones

100+ colors available including domestic and imported granite. Standard monument dimensions in stock; custom sizes fabricated to specification. Available for dealer and wholesale orders.

Sandblasting Services

In-house precision sandblasting for names, dates, emblems, and decorative patterns. Consistent depth and finish quality. Available as a service for dealer-supplied blanks.

Custom Etching

Laser and hand etching for portraits, landscapes, and detailed artwork. Offered as an add-on finishing service for monument dealers and resellers.

Custom Design & Fabrication

Submit custom specifications or design files. Mockups provided before production. Available for trade accounts placing repeat or bulk orders.

The Significance of Memorials

A memorial transcends time, preserving the legacy of those who touched our lives. At Angel Granites, we understand the weight of this responsibility. Our granite monuments stand as enduring symbols of love, remembrance, and respect.

Granite, a geological marvel, has fascinated humanity for centuries. As civilization evolved, we harnessed its beauty and strength. Angel Granites proudly contributes to this legacy, creating monuments that resonate with the aesthetic sensibilities of Americans.
